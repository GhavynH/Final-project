  • Past

    I grew up in the mountains, in a community called Shaver Springs. I went to Pine Ridge Elementary, with a gradually dwindling passion for education. I always was intrigued by technology, I have enjoyed tinkering since a young age.

    I went to Sierra High School. I was active in most of the music groups. In my first two years, I played sports, but gradually I stopped being active in HS. In my senior year, I switched to independent study, and graduated early.

    After highschool my life has been crazy, but I will condense it to save you time. Worked minimum wage jobs, breaking my back for no pay or acknowledgement. I was tired of that life so I became an EMT and worked that for a few years. I realized the work life of an EMT was too much, and when I got laid off due to covid, I decided to change the direction I am taking my life. I decided to learn coding to provide for myself while I get my masters in Psych.

  • The way I try to exist

    Alright, I am gonna try to do this without sounding like a raging narcissist, or tooting my own horn too much. Buckle up, and lets go.

    My life Philosophy is very simple when you break it down. We all live once, so why not spend our lives doing the most good that we possibly can? I believe in doing what I can to help ensure other people have good days/weeks/months. I believe in not taking life too seriously. If we all live once, we should make sure we live our lives in a way that make us happy. I like to just have fun and do my thing, getting caught up on the little things isn't worth it. On top of it all, I believe in kindness. I am not the only one that only has one life, so I try my best to make sure that my presence helps elevate others lives, rather than bring them down.
